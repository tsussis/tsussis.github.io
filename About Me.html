About Me

Hi, I’m Tyler! I am a Data Science / Info Science undergrad at the University of Wisconsin–Madison I have a passion for creative storytelling through video, photography, and design. My favorite works blend technical skill with visual intuition; from cinematic video edits and photo composition to data visualizations.


What to Expect

On this website, I showcase my favorite projects and influences. I make graphic designs using adobe photoshop & illistrator, motion graphics using adobe after effects in combination with Davinci Resolve, and data visualizations using Tableau.
